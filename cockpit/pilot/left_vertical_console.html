Landing Gear Control Panel

Control panel for landing gear operation and emergency stores jettison.

Landing Gear Handle

The LDG GEAR handle (1) selects landing gear UP or DOWN.

For emergency extension with the handle in DOWN, push the handle in, rotate it clockwise, and pull outward. This releases a compressed nitrogen charge to extend the gear.

Down Lock Override

The DOWN LOCK ORIDE indicator (2) moves down by solenoid to indicate weight on wheels.

The indicator may be lifted to override the signal.

Hydraulic Isolation Switch

The HYD ISOL switch (3) isolates landing gear, nosewheel steering, and wheel brakes from the combined hydraulic system.

The switch is automatically moved to T.O./LDG when the landing gear handle is in the DOWN position.

  • FLT â€” In-flight operation. Isolates listed systems.
  • T.O./LDG â€” Takeoff and landing operation. Connects listed systems.

The transition light (4) illuminates when landing gear position does not match handle position.
